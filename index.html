About Me

Business Analyst & System Engineer

Hi, I’m Pramita — a data enthusiast who loves connecting business goals with data-driven insights. With over 7 years of experience across analytics, solution design, and systems engineering, I’ve learned that great analysis isn’t just about numbers — it’s about understanding people, processes, and potential. I enjoy working with SQL, Python, Power BI, and Azure to turn data into clear, impactful stories.

Outside of work, I’m passionate about exploring the world — having travelled across 4 continents and over 20 countries so far. I’m especially drawn to ancient ruins, mountain trails for hiking, and the tranquility of ocean snorkeling. To unwind, I enjoy swimming, creating simple art through drawing and coloring, and immersing myself in Egyptology as well as ancient and modern history. I also love staying active through tennis and racquetball.
